Making Resumes

This page will help guide you making a resume

When making a resume as a teenager, highlight your transferable skills like communication, teamwork, and problem-solving. Include any volunteer work or community service to showcase your experience. Keep the format simple and easy to read, and tailor your resume to each specific job you apply for. Adding a brief personal statement about your goals and why you're a good fit can also make a strong impression.
